This map shows vaccination rates in New York City by ZIP code. Each dot represents a mass vaccination site –– click to learn more.

Source: https://www1.nyc.gov/site/doh/covid/covid-19-data-vaccines.page

About the data: The data comes from the Citywide Immunization Registry, which collects data from immunizing facilities.

Fully vaccinated: Percentage of the adult population that has received both doses of a two-dose COVID-19 vaccine series.
